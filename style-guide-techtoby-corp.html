2 — Components / Modal

Backdrop: rgba(0,0,0,0.3) + backdrop-filter:blur(8px). Content scales in from 0.9 to 1.0. Add/remove .show to toggle. Two variants: the standard reusable Modal and the more-opaque Settings ModalOverlay.

Modal Title

Modal body content is injected here. Supports any HTML — forms, lists, text.

<div class="Modal" id="appModal"> <div class="ModalContent"> <div class="ModalHeader"> <h3 id="modalTitle">Title</h3> <button class="CloseModalBtn">&times;</button> </div> <div class="ModalBody" id="modalBody"></div> </div> </div> <!-- Toggle: modal.classList.toggle('show') -->
